1 Arrival & First Lizard Sunset

Depart Bracknell at 10:30 AM to bypass the notorious weekend bottlenecks on the A30. Arrive at Lizard Point by 4:00 PM, settle into your accommodation, and step straight onto the southernmost cliff paths of mainland Britain.

Sunset Hotspot (Approx 8:15 PM): Head past the white twin towers of Lizard Lighthouse to Polpeor Cove. The heather is in brilliant purple bloom against dark serpentine coastal rocks.

Driving Logistics:

  • Distance: 245 Miles (via M4, M5, A30, A3083)
  • Time: ~4 hours 45 mins net driving

2 Cove Wonders & Rescued Marine Seals

Today involves timing Kynance Cove's spectacular sands at low tide, moving to historic Mullion Harbour, and spending the afternoon watching rescued baby grey seals recover at the Sanctuary.

Universal Credit Savings Unleashed: By presenting your Universal Credit statement and photo ID at the Cornish Seal Sanctuary, you pay just £15 per adult / £7.50 per child rather than the standard £39.50/£15 gate entry fee! Saving your family of four over £64.00 instantly.

Step-by-Step Schedule:

08:30 AM — Kynance Cove (Tide Critical)

Hike the wild cliff trail down. Golden sands and rockpooling. Café is cash/App only.

12:30 PM — Mullion Cove & Village

Explore the ancient serpentine harbor walls. Pick up Cornish pasties in Mullion village.

02:30 PM — Cornish Seal Sanctuary, Gweek

Watch feeding displays. Buggy friendly wood trails and gorgeous Helford river views.
